aPPLEbYTES sessions

The aPPLEbYTES sessions are a series of HD, high quality audio videos that involve either acoustic performances featuring NYC artists or electronic duos. All the takes are recorded in real time, capturing the fleeting nature of a live performance, in Aleks Studio in Harlem, NYC.
